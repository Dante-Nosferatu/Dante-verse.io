Synopsis

At the beginning of World War II, scientists on the Nazi side were experimenting with human and animal bodies, seeking immortality and to increase capabilities beyond the unthinkable, something similar to the super soldier serum. However, something went wrong during the experiments, and the human and animal test subjects went mad and ate each other. But the most interesting thing the scientists observed was that the test subjects concentrated on drinking blood rather than eating flesh. That was the starting point to begin creating a serum... which would later become a virus.
