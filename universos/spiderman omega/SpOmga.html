spider-man

General Information

  • Universe Name: Spider-man Omega
  • Universe Type: 3 in 1

Universe Characteristics

This universe contains three universes, each with three Spider-Men: Amazing, 2099, and Noir.

Amazing : This is the main one of the three. It would be exactly the same as a Spider-Man from Earth-616, but with a major change that you'll see as you read on.

2099: Clearly based on Michael O'Hara, but it's not him, but his son, Jake O'Hara. This whole story is part of My Hero Academia.

Noir: Its more specific name would be "Dead" because this Spider-Man kills and is bloodthirsty, similar to the original version, but worse.
